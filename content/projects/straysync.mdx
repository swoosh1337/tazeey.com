# StraySync

StraySync helps you locate and track stray animals in your area. Pin locations, upload photos, and connect with others to help our furry friends.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Map**: View stray animal sightings on a map with real-time updates
- **Add Animal Sightings**: Upload photos and mark the exact location of stray cats and dogs
- **Animal Details**: View photos, descriptions, and get directions to stray animal locations
- **Proximity Notifications**: Get alerts when you're near a stray animal location
- **Customizable Settings**: Set notification radius and time frame preferences
- **Directions**: Get directions to stray animal locations directly from the app

Whether you're reuniting pets with owners or alerting local rescuers, every pin brings us closer to a world where no stray goes unnoticed.
