# Meditopia

Experience deep relaxation with our Meditation app. Set a custom meditation timer, journal your sessions, and track your progress and streaks. Find calm, build consistency, and deepen your practice.

## Key Features

- **Meditation Timer**: Customize your meditation duration with our intuitive timer
- **Journal**: Record your thoughts and experiences after each session
- **Heart Rate Tracking**: Monitor your heart rate during meditation sessions
- **Streaks**: Build consistency with meditation streaks and progress tracking

Meditopia helps you develop a consistent meditation practice, making it easier to incorporate mindfulness into your daily routine. The app provides a simple yet powerful set of tools to enhance your meditation experience.
