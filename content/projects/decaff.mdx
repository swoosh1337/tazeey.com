# Decaff

Track your caffeine intake and balance sleep effortlessly. Decaff helps you track and manage your daily caffeine intake from coffee, tea, and various other caffeinated beverages. Monitor consumption, visualize trends, and receive insights on how caffeine affects your sleep and energy levels.

## Key Features

- **Log Your Drinks Easily**: Quickly record coffee, tea, soda, and energy drinks with our extensive database
- **Visual Insights**: View daily, weekly, and monthly trends with intuitive charts and graphs
- **Track Your History**: Keep a detailed log of your caffeine intake and monitor consumption patterns
- **AI-Driven Analysis**: Get personalized insights on how caffeine affects your mood and productivity
- **Sleep Optimization**: Understand how your caffeine intake affects your sleep patterns and quality
- **Smart Reminders**: Stay on track with notifications to log drinks throughout the day

Whether you're cutting back or optimizing energy, Decaff keeps you in control of your caffeine habits.
